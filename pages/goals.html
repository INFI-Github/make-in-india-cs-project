Objectives of Make in India Campaign

  • To transform India into a global design and manufacturing hub.
  • To create a world-class infrastructure for manufacturing.
  • To promote innovation, R&D and skill development.
  • To build a self-reliant India.
  • To make India a global hub for manufacturing.
  • To create a skilled workforce.
  • To promote start-ups and entrepreneurship.
  • To promote Make in India in the global market.
  • To introduce new initiatives for the promotion of foreign direct investment
